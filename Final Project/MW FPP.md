##Overview
- For my final project, I will be making a portfolio website desgined to present my personal brand as a music producer. The site will feature embedded music (beats, songs I produced, and collaborations), a short bio, visual branding, and contact information for networking/business inquiries. My goal is to create something that feels both professional and artistically unique. In addition to this I want to also incorporate a shop secion where vistors can purchase drum kits, loop kits, sound kits, or production templates. This allows my site to also serve as a platform where I can sell direct to audience and help build a business around my music. 

- None of this work overlaps with any classes I am currently taking, but the idea stemmed from my professinal development class where I had to create a portfolio website for an assignment.
## GOALS
- Have a published website
- Have a functioning website (shop functions well, pages look aesthetically pleasing, All embedded songs/beats work)
- Be able to fully understand the coding around my website so I am able to freely make adjustments/changes whenever anything needs to be updated. 

- I think for this project I can get most of it done. The functioning website is the most important thing I want for this project, as long as it works I am satisfied and I can always make adjustments in the future.
