DANCE
Stand up-right with your head pointing to the sky and hands to your side 
slowly look down until your eyes point straight ahead 
bring both arms out infront of you with your hands closed into fists 
bring your left arm up until it slightly above your head and bring your right arm down towards your knee
Alternate left and right arm positions going up and down and always move them at the same time 
while arms are moving up and down in opposite directions bend your knees SLIGHTLY and then straighten them 
now continuously alternate moving your arms up and down in opposite directions and keep Slightly bending your knees and straightening them 
the bird is the word.
